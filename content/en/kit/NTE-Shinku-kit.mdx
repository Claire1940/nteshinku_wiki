<Section icon={Swords} title="NTE Shinku Kit and Combat Mechanics Explained">

The **NTE Shinku kit** introduces a highly aggressive, dual-form martial arts playstyle to Neverness to Everness. As an S-Class Cosmos Esper with a Condensate-type Arc, Shinku transitions from a controlled builder phase into a devastating, high-speed berserker. Understanding how her two forms interact is vital to maximizing her damage output on the battlefield.

| Form | Core Gauge | Key Actions | Combat Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Form** | Defiant Spirit | Basic attacks, Dodge counters | Builds energy to ready her Ultimate |
| **Rampage Form** | Fading Reason | Enhanced strikes, Tidal Assault | Unleashes AoE nukes and high-speed combos |

### Normal Form: Building Defiant Spirit
In her standard state, Shinku utilizes specialized fighting techniques to deliver up to five consecutive strikes. Landing her third or fifth hit, or holding down the attack button during these sequences, triggers an altered combat state that generates **Defiant Spirit**. 

### Rampage Form: Surging Crimson
Once her Defiant Spirit gauge is full, Shinku can activate her Ultimate to enter the **Surging Crimson** state. This transformation lasts for 13 seconds, completely replacing her basic attacks and switch skills with enhanced, high-interruption variants. During this window, she builds **Fading Reason**. Before the form expires, Shinku can consume her remaining gauge to unleash a massive, high-multiplier AoE nuke.

<Callout type="tip" title="Open World Exploration Tip">
Shinku's rampage state isn't just for combat. In the open world, holding down the dodge button allows her to enter her high-speed sprint mode. Additionally, she glides using unique purple energy wings instead of a standard glider.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Shinku Build: Arcs and Cartridges">

To optimize the NTE Shinku kit, you must focus on stats that complement her high-frequency strikes and critical hits. Because her damage scales heavily during her Ultimate window, prioritizing Crit Rate, Crit Damage, and Cosmos Damage is essential.

### Top Arc Recommendations
Shinku's signature Arc, **Blushing Mirage**, is her absolute best-in-slot option. It provides unmatched offensive multipliers tailored directly to her kit's burst windows. However, strong F2P and alternative options are available.

| Arc Name | Rarity | Type | Key Combat Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blushing Mirage** | S-Class | Condensate | Increases Cosmos damage; ignores 12% enemy DEF after Ultimate |
| **Fluff of Ferocity** | S-Class | Condensate | High offensive stats; synergizes with CRIT-heavy kits |
| **Camellia Society** | S-Class | Condensate | Solid alternative offering stable offensive stat boosts |
| **What's Desired** | A-Class | F2P Friendly | Excellent budget pick with a built-in CRIT Rate substat |

### Cartridge and Module Setups
For her gear, the **Lost Radiance** S-Class set is the premier choice. This set enhances her native elemental profile and scales her burst potential.

- **Recommended Set**: Lost Radiance (S-Class)
- **Main Stats**: Cosmos DMG% (Cartridge), CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG (Modules)
- **Substat Priority**: CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > Universal DMG% > ATK%

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Investment Priority">
While Shinku's entire kit contributes to her overall DPS, her Ultimate holds the highest damage multipliers. Prioritize upgrading her skills in this order: **Ultimate > Skill = Basic Attack > Support Skill**.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play Shinku: Step-by-Step Rotation">

Unlocking the full potential of the NTE Shinku kit requires precise execution of her dual-form rotation. Follow these steps to maximize your damage windows in tough combat encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Combat Stacking">
    Before entering a fight, remain out of combat for a few seconds to passively stack **Deterrent Gaze**. In the open world, you can comfortably start a battle with a maximum of 8 stacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unleash Instant Strike">
    Initiate the battle with an immediate basic attack. This consumes your Deterrent Gaze stacks to trigger **Instant Strike**, dealing massive damage and instantly restoring Ultimate, Skill, and Cycle energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Execute her 5-hit basic attack string. Hold down the attack button during the 3rd, 4th, or 5th hit to trigger her Altered Combat Arts, rapidly filling your **Defiant Spirit** gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter Surging Crimson">
    Once your gauge is full, activate her Ultimate. This transforms her moveset for 13 seconds, giving you access to high-interruption, fast-moving AoE attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unleash the Finisher">
    Before the 13-second transformation window closes, execute her enhanced skill to consume all accumulated **Fading Reason** and trigger her ultimate nuke finisher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
