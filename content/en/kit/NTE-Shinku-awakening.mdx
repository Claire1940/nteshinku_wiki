<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Deep Dive: Every Awakening Level Explained">

To understand why certain milestones are prioritized, we must look at how each level alters her combat flow and damage output.

### A1: Crimson Piercer (DEF Ignore)
*Effect: Shinku's damage ignores 12% of the target's DEF.*
This is a straightforward, highly efficient passive multiplier. Because DEF-ignore mechanics scale exceptionally well against high-level endgame bosses, A1 represents a substantial early power spike for minimal investment.

### A2: Pre-Dawn Training Grounds (Res Shred)
*Effect: Reduces the target's Cosmos Resistance by 10% for 20s after the Ultimate hits.*
A2 acts as a self-enabling debuff. Since Shinku deals pure Cosmos damage during her post-Ultimate burst window, reducing enemy resistance directly translates to an overall team and solo damage increase during her primary DPS window.

### A3: Sunset Beach (Off-Field Mechanics)
*Effect: Keeps Menacing Gaze active after switching characters and allows Shinku to gain Menacing Gaze stacks while off-field.*
This is Shinku's most critical quality-of-life upgrade. At base, Shinku must remain on-field without attacking to build Menacing Gaze stacks, which slows down combat. A3 completely removes this restriction, letting her build stacks while your support characters set up reactions, priming her for massive swap-in burst attacks.

### A4: Intimidating Focus (Stack Cap & Stun)
*Effect: Increases the Menacing Gaze stack cap to 16. Enhances Instant Strike to inflict Forced Knockback, brief stun, and an extra 20% damage instance.*
A4 works in perfect synergy with A3. Since you are now generating stacks off-field effortlessly, raising the cap to 16 allows Shinku to execute an incredibly powerful, crowd-controlling counterattack immediately upon taking the field.

### A5: Crimson Fortitude (Interruption & Heal)
*Effect: Increases DEF by 50% and Interruption Resistance by 15% in Ultimate state. Heals Shinku based on ATK when using Enhanced Skills.*
While this makes Shinku incredibly tanky and comfortable to play, it does not contribute to her offensive ceiling. Skilled players who utilize her Perfect Dodge and parry mechanics will find less value here, making it a low-priority tier.

### A6: Unruly Execution (The Ultimate Finisher)
*Effect: Increases damage ratios for Enhanced Skill and Ultimate by 30% during her Ultimate state, and allows these skills to pull in targets within range.*
This is the pinnacle of Shinku's build. It turns her into a self-sufficient vortex of destruction, combining massive raw damage multipliers with a built-in gathering mechanic that groups enemies automatically.

<Callout type="warning" title="Budget Warning">
While A6 is incredibly powerful, it is highly expensive. Only pursue A6 if you are fully committed to making Shinku your primary carry for the foreseeable future.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Shinku's Optimal Combat Rotation">

To make the most of Shinku's Awakenings, you must execute her dual-form combat loop correctly. Follow this step-by-step guide to maximize her on-field uptime.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Up Support Buffs Off-Field">
    Start the battle with your support characters (such as Hathor and Jiuyuan) to trigger reaction elements like Lakshana and Anima. If you have **A3 unlocked**, Shinku is actively building Menacing Gaze stacks in the background during this time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Swap In and Trigger Instant Strike">
    Switch to Shinku. Immediately execute her Basic Attack to consume her accumulated Menacing Gaze stacks, unleashing a high-damage, high-energy Instant Strike that rapidly builds her Defiant Spirit gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Use her fast martial arts strings, parries, and Redirect Skills to top off her Defiant Spirit. Perfect Dodges will award additional stacks and trigger powerful counter-kicks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Crimson Ascension">
    Once her Defiant Spirit is fully charged, activate her Ultimate to enter her transformed state. She gains enhanced basic attacks, increased movement speed, and removes the cooldown on her skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute the Enhanced Burst Chain">
    Spam her enhanced basic attacks to charge her skill, then unleash her Enhanced Skill. Repeat this loop up to 5 times, then finish with her Ultimate dashes and final high-damage energy blast before swapping out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
