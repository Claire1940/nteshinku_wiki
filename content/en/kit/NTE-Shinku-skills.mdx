### Normal Form Skill Breakdown

In her standard state, Shinku acts as an on-field driver, accumulating energy to fuel her transformation. Her basic attacks are fast, physical-looking martial arts strikes that deal Cosmos damage.

| Skill Name | Skill Type | Combat Description | Resource Generated |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Special Combat Arts** | Basic Attack | Delivers up to 5 consecutive physical/martial strikes dealing Cosmos DMG. | Generates **Defiant Spirit**. |
| **Special Combat Arts — Altered** | Branch Attack | Hold Basic Attack during the 3rd, 4th, or 5th hit of her normal string. | Generates high amount of **Defiant Spirit**. |
| **High-Speed Breach** | Redirect Skill | Shinku rockets forward, performing a rapid multi-hit flurry of Cosmos DMG. | Generates **Defiant Spirit**. |
| **Instant Victory** | Dodge Counter | Triggered by tapping Basic Attack after a Perfect Dodge, executing a vicious flying kick. | Grants **Defiant Spirit** and drains enemy Break Gauge. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Stance Prep Loop">
Do not just mindlessly spam normal attacks. Always trigger **Special Combat Arts — Altered** by holding down the attack button during the 3rd or 5th strike. This is the fastest way to max out your Defiant Spirit meter and ready your transformation.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play Shinku: Step-by-Step Rotation">

To maximize Shinku's damage output, you must integrate her off-field preparation, on-field resource building, and transformation window into a seamless rotation. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Off-Field Stacking (Menacing Gaze)">
    Keep Shinku off-field or avoid attacking for 2 seconds to trigger **Menacing Gaze**. She will passively build up to 8 stacks (or 16 stacks with higher Awakenings).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initiate with Instant Strike">
    Swap Shinku onto the field and execute her first Basic Attack. This consumes all Menacing Gaze stacks to unleash a massive **Instant Strike**, instantly generating a huge chunk of Defiant Spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Perform her standard 5-hit basic string, ensuring you hold the button on the 3rd or 5th hit to execute her **Altered** branch attack. Mix in her Redirect Skill, **High-Speed Breach**, to fill the gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter Surging Crimson">
    Once your Defiant Spirit gauge is full, cast her Ultimate, **Crimson Fury**, to transform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sustained Burst Window">
    Spam **Tidal Assault** (Enhanced Basic Attacks) to build Fading Reason, then immediately spend it on her enhanced skill, **Crimson Blade from the Heavens**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Execute the Crimson Finisher">
    Just before her 13-second transformation ends, continuously tap her Ultimate button to execute **Crimson Judgment** and cap it off with the massive **Dragon Flame Verdict**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upgrading NTE Shinku skills requires a balanced approach, but prioritizing her primary damage-dealing tools will yield the highest return on investment.

### Skill Leveling Priority Guide

1. **Ultimate: Crimson Fury (Priority 1)**: Directly increases the multiplier of her transformation damage, Crimson Judgment, and Dragon Flame Verdict.
2. **Basic Attack: Special Combat Arts (Priority 2)**: Boosts her standard melee attacks and her transformed Tidal Assault strikes, which make up a massive portion of her on-field uptime.
3. **Redirect Skill: High-Speed Breach (Priority 3)**: Improves her gap closer and the downward claw slam during her transformed state.
4. **Support Skill: Fierce Knee Strike (Priority 4)**: Can be left at lower levels as it is only used during quick-swap Esper Cycles.
