<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding the NTE Shinku Role and Combat Kit">

The **NTE Shinku role** in Neverness to Everness (NTE) is that of a premier, on-field Cosmos main DPS. As a member of the Bureau of Anomaly Control's Containment Strike Unit 2, this reserved dragon girl unleashes devastating hand-to-hand combat styles. Her gameplay centers around a highly aggressive dual-form rotation that rewards players who manage her unique energy gauges effectively.

Unlike standard characters who can rely solely on passive ultimate energy regeneration, Shinku must actively engage in combat to unlock her true damage potential. 

| Combat State | Gauge Mechanic | Primary Attack Style | Key Combat Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Form** | Builds **Defiant Spirit** via basic/charged attacks | Fast martial arts strikes dealing Cosmos damage | On-field builder & setup phase |
| **Surging Crimson** | Consumes Defiant Spirit, builds **Fading Reason** | Transformed **Tidal Assault** strings & massive AoE | Main burst DPS & defense ignore phase |

### Key Combat Passives & Mechanics
- **Deterrent Gaze**: Generates stacks for a burst damage boost when she is in a combat state but temporarily not actively attacking.
- **High-Speed Breach**: Her Redirect skill that allows her to rocket directly toward targets to maintain offensive pressure.
- **Charge Cycle Enhancement**: Her support skill provides a massive team benefit. When the active character triggers a Charge reaction to gain Ultimate energy, all off-field allies gain the exact same amount of energy. While Shinku is active and in a Charge state, she gains a stacking 5% ATK buff (up to 10 stacks) and triggers extra AoE damage.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Shinku loses all of her active on-field ATK buffs when she is switched out. To maximize her DPS role, ensure that all support buffs from your off-field characters are fully active before bringing Shinku on-field to initiate her Surging Crimson burst window.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Shinku Build: Arcs and Cartridges">

To optimize the NTE Shinku role, you must equip her with Arcs and Cartridges that scale her Cosmos damage and critical strike capabilities. Because her kit naturally features high attack speeds and multi-hit strings, she gains incredible value from critical hits and defense-ignoring modifiers.

### Best Arcs (Weapons) for Shinku
Shinku utilizes a Condensate-type Arc (previously referred to as Synthesis-type). 

| Arc Name | Rarity | Primary Synergies & Effects | Role Suitability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blushing Mirage** | S-Class | Increases Cosmos/Light Psychic DMG by 30% after Ultimate; ignores 12% of enemy DEF. | **Best-in-Slot (BiS)** |
| **Fluff of Ferocity** | S-Class | Increases Crit DMG by 4% per critical hit (stacks up to 10 times for a total of +40% Crit DMG). | Excellent Battle Pass alternative |
| **Camellia Society** | S-Class | Provides high base offensive stats suited for Cosmos damage dealers. | Strong secondary S-Class option |
| **What's Desired** | A-Class | Provides a highly valuable Crit Rate substat to ease early-game gearing. | Best F2P Option |

### Cartridge and Stat Priorities
Your target benchmarks for Shinku are **70% Crit Rate** and **150% Crit DMG**. Once you secure these parameters through sub-stats, focus entirely on scaling her Cosmos damage.

<Section icon={Target} title="Best Team Compositions for Shinku">

Since Shinku is locked into an on-field main DPS role, her team compositions must be built entirely around supporting her on-field presence. Shinku relies heavily on triggering **Charge reactions** (combining Lakshana and Anima elements) to quickly generate Ultimate energy and stack her passive ATK buffs.

### Top Shinku Team Configurations

| Team Archetype | Main DPS | Burst / Sub-DPS | Support / Enabler | Flex / Healer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Premium Charge Team** | **Shinku** (Cosmos) | **Hathor** (Lakshana) | **Jiuyuan** (Anima) | **Iroi** (Anima) |
| **F2P/Standard Team** | **Shinku** (Cosmos) | **Hathor** (Lakshana) | **Esper Zero** (Cosmos) | **Edgar** (Cosmos) |

### Synergy Breakdown
- **Hathor**: Essential for applying the Lakshana element and extending the duration of the **Remora** effect. This keeps the Charge cycle active, while also granting Shinku a flat 10% Crit Rate buff.
- **Iroi**: Arriving in Phase 2 of Version 1.2, Iroi provides top-tier Anima support, healing, and offensive buffs that perfectly round out Shinku's premium elemental cycle.
- **Esper Zero**: A fantastic Cosmos support who helps fill the Cosmos Esper Cycle gauge. Zero's support skill also heals the active character for 50% of Zero's Base ATK whenever Shinku gains Ultimate energy, offering a reliable safety net for Shinku's aggressive playstyle.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Combat Rotation Guide">

To master the NTE Shinku role in actual combat, you must execute a strict rotation that builds her gauges, triggers elemental reactions, and unleashes her massive burst damage during her transformed state.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Up Off-Field Elements">
    Start the fight with your support characters. Use **Hathor** to apply Lakshana and activate the Remora effect, then swap to your Anima support (**Jiuyuan** or **Iroi**) to trigger a Charge reaction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Swap to Shinku. Use her basic martial arts strings (up to 5 consecutive strikes) and her Redirect skill, **High-Speed Breach**, to quickly fill her **Defiant Spirit Gauge**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Crimson Fury">
    Once the gauge is full, cast her Ultimate, **Crimson Fury**. This consumes her Defiant Spirit and transitions her into the empowered **Surging Crimson** (Rampage) state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Tidal Assault">
    While in Surging Crimson, execute her transformed **Tidal Assault** attacks to deal heavy AoE Cosmos damage and build up her **Fading Reason Gauge**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Cast Ultimate Dragonflame Verdict">
    Before her Surging Crimson state ends, consume all accumulated Fading Reason to activate her enhanced skill, culminating in the massive **Ultimate Dragonflame Verdict** nuke to wipe out remaining enemies.
  </Step>
</Steps>
