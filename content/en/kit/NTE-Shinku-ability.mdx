<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Shinku Combat Rotation">

To maximize Shinku's on-field DPS, you must master the transition between her base form and her Surging Crimson state. Follow this optimal combat rotation to shred enemy shields and unleash massive Cosmos damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Start the fight in her base form. Perform her 5-hit **Special Combat Arts** combo. Hold down the attack button during the 3rd, 4th, or 5th strike to trigger her Altered attacks for extra energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use High-Speed Breach">
    Cast her Normal Skill, **High-Speed Breach**, to close distances. This deals multi-hit Cosmos damage and rapidly fills the remainder of her **Defiant Spirit** gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Surging Crimson">
    Once the gauge is completely full, press your Ultimate key to activate **Crimson Fury**. She will consume all her Defiant Spirit, deal massive initial AoE damage, and enter her 13-second transformed state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Transformed Combo">
    While transformed, spam her enhanced basic attack, **Tidal Assault**. Use her enhanced skill, **Crimson Blade from the Heavens**, whenever it is off cooldown to maintain high DPS and heal her back to full health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unleash the Finisher">
    Right before her 13-second transformation window expires, continuously tap the Ultimate button to execute **Crimson Judgment**. This triggers her rapid-dash execution strikes, dealing devastating final blows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Deterrent Gaze Mechanic">
When exploring the open world, Shinku gains stacks of **Deterrent Gaze** every second she is out of combat or hasn't attacked. She can enter combat with a full 8 stacks, allowing her first strike to deal massive instant damage and instantly refund a portion of her Ultimate energy.
</Callout>
