<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Playing Shinku">

Shinku relies on a specific rhythm in combat to cycle her forms and maintain high damage output. Follow these steps to master her playstyle:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Start the battle with Shinku on-field. Use her 5-hit Basic Attack strings and dodge counters to rapidly fill her Defiant Spirit Gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unleash Crimson Fury">
    Once the gauge is full, activate her Ultimate to enter the Surging Crimson rampage state. This transforms her moveset into high-impact AoE martial arts strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stack Fading Reason">
    During her rampage state, continue landing enhanced attacks to build up her Fading Reason Gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Finishing Nuke">
    Before the Surging Crimson state ends, use her enhanced skill to consume all Fading Reason stacks, unleashing a massive Cosmos damage nuke.
  </Step>
</Steps>
