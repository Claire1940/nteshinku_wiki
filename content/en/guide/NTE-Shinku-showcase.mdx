<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Build: Blushing Mirage and Arc Priority">

A proper **NTE Shinku showcase** requires the right equipment. Shinku is designed to utilize Condensate-type Arcs (formerly known as Synthesis-type). Her signature weapon, the **Blushing Mirage**, is available during the same July 8–29 window through the "Resolve Special" Arc Research Program. While other S-Class Arcs exist, the Blushing Mirage is tailor-made for her Cosmos Esper abilities.

| Arc Name | Rank | Type | Compatibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blushing Mirage** | S-Class | Condensate | **Best in Slot** (Signature) |
| **Reality Refuge** | S-Class | Defensive | Good for survivability |
| **Youthful Fantasy** | S-Class | Utility | Alternative for support builds |
| **Camellia Society** | S-Class | Balanced | General-purpose S-Class |
| **Raging Flames** | S-Class | Offensive | High-risk DPS alternative |

When managing your resources, prioritize the character first. Once Shinku is secured, use Tri-Keys or Resolve Keys to target the Blushing Mirage. The Resolve Special program allows you to focus your pulls, reducing the chance of obtaining off-banner Arcs like "Good Boy's Grand Adventure."

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Strategy">
If you are a F2P player, don't feel pressured to pull the signature Arc immediately. Shinku performs exceptionally well with high-refinement A-Class Condensate Arcs until you can save enough Resolve Keys for a guaranteed Blushing Mirage.
</Callout>
