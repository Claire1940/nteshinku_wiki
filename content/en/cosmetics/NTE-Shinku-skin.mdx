<Section icon={Target} title="Unlock Guide: Student of Terrasea Banner Skin">

The **Student of Terrasea** is perhaps the most sought-after **NTE Shinku skin** because it is tied directly to the "Before the Dawn" Limited Board. Unlike shop skins, this outfit requires participation in the gacha system. It represents Shinku's academic background and offers a scholarly yet mysterious aesthetic that resonates with her "awkward but dependable" personality.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Limited Board">
    Complete the main story quest **Prologue - A New Destination - Head to Eibon Antique Shop** to unlock the banner menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accumulate Pulls">
    Use Solid Dice or Annulith to roll on the **Before the Dawn** Limited Board. The banner runs from July 8 to July 29, 2026.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Resolve Perks">
    Navigate to the Resolve Perks menu to track your milestone progress. The system guarantees specific rewards at set intervals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim the Outfit">
    Once you reach the **200-roll milestone**, you can manually claim the **Student of Terrasea** outfit from the rewards screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

It is important to note that the Student of Terrasea can also be obtained randomly by landing on the matching tile on the Limited Board before reaching the 200-roll guarantee. However, the milestone system ensures that dedicated players will eventually secure the skin regardless of their luck.

| Milestone | Reward Type | Item Name | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **50 Rolls** | Glider Livery | **Overcast Canopy** | Exclusive grey/purple glider skin |
| **120 Rolls** | Vehicle Livery | **Hidden Dragon** | Blizzard-V4 custom livery |
| **200 Rolls** | Character Skin | **Student of Terrasea** | Shinku's featured banner outfit |

<Callout type="warning" title="Banner Expiry">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Free-to-Play and Progression Cosmetics">

NTE provides several avenues for players to earn an **NTE Shinku skin** without spending premium currency. These are tied to gameplay progression, character loyalty, and seasonal events. The most notable among these is the Bond-related outfit, which rewards players for spending time with Shinku in Hethereau.

**Bond Level Rewards:**
The **Labyrinth Lily** outfit is a reward for reaching the maximum Bond Level with Shinku. This process involves utilizing the new interaction features introduced in Version 1.2.

1. **City Hangouts**: Complete Shinku's dedicated story episodes to earn significant Bond XP.
2. **Home Time**: Invite Shinku to your home base for personal interactions and dialogue choices.
3. **Text Messages**: Respond to Shinku's messages on the in-game phone to maintain your relationship.
4. **Combat Usage**: Including Shinku in your active team during missions provides a steady trickle of Bond points.
