<Section icon={Swords} title="Build Basics & Signature Arc: Blushing Mirage">

While the **NTE Shinku summer skin** provides the visual flair, her combat effectiveness relies on her signature Arc, **Blushing Mirage**. This S-Class Condensate-type Arc is designed specifically to synergize with Shinku's Cosmos Esper abilities.

| Arc Name | Rarity | Type | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blushing Mirage** | S-Class | Condensate | **Shinku (Best-in-Slot)** |
| **Reality Refuge** | S-Class | Defense | Tanky Builds |
| **Youthful Fantasy** | S-Class | Support | Utility/Buffing |
| **Raging Flames** | S-Class | Attack | Pure DPS |

The **Blushing Mirage** Arc is available through the **Resolve Special Arc Research Program**. Much like the character banner, this program uses **Tri-Keys** or **Resolve Keys**. If you are a low-spender, prioritize the character first, as Shinku's base kit is functional even with A-Class Arcs, though her signature weapon significantly boosts her protective capabilities.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not spend your keys on the Standard Arc Research Program if you want Blushing Mirage. Only the **Resolve Special** pool features the limited-time rate-up for Shinku's signature gear.
</Callout>
