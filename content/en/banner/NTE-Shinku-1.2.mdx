<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Mechanics & Skill Rotation">

Shinku features a dual-form combat style that transitions between building resources in her base form and unleashing high-damage AoE strikes in her empowered form.

### Core Combat Resources

1. **Defiant Spirit Gauge**: Built by using basic attacks and dodge counters in her normal form. Unlike other characters, Shinku cannot cast her Ultimate solely through standard Ultimate Energy; she must fully charge this gauge first.
2. **Fading Reason Gauge**: Built up by landing basic attacks during her Surging Crimson (Rampage) state. 

### Step-by-Step Combat Rotation

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Execute her standard 5-hit basic attack combo, holding down the attack button during the final three hits to trigger extended strikes and charge her Defiant Spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Surging Crimson">
    Once the Defiant Spirit gauge is full, activate her Ultimate to enter the Surging Crimson (Rampage) form, altering her basic attacks to deal high-velocity AoE damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stack Fading Reason">
    Perform her enhanced basic attacks during the Rampage state to quickly accumulate Fading Reason.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash the Finish Nuke">
    Near the end of her Surging Crimson state, activate the newly unlocked dash-nuke skill to consume all Fading Reason and execute a massive final strike.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Callout>
