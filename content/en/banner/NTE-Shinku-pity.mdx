<Section icon={Target} title="NTE Shinku Pity and Banner Mechanics">

The arrival of Version 1.2, titled "999 Nights," introduces the highly anticipated S-Class character Shinku. Understanding the **NTE Shinku pity** structure is essential for players looking to optimize their resource expenditure. Unlike traditional gacha systems that only focus on character acquisition, Neverness to Everness utilizes a "Limited Board" mechanic for its featured banners. This system incorporates specific roll milestones that guarantee cosmetic and functional rewards alongside the character pulls.

- **Character Reveal**: A deep dive into Shinku's "Edge of Dreams" aesthetic and combat animations.

The "Before the Dawn" Limited Board is the primary source for Shinku. Accessing this board requires players to have completed the prologue quest "A New Destination - Head to Eibon Antique Shop." Once unlocked, players can use Annulith or Solid Dice to participate. The banner features a tiered reward system where specific thresholds provide guaranteed items, effectively serving as a pity system for high-value cosmetics.

| Milestone | Reward Type | Item Name |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **50 Rolls** | Glider Livery | **Overcast Canopy** |
| **120 Rolls** | Vehicle Livery | **Hidden Dragon (Blizzard-V4)** |
| **200 Rolls** | Exclusive Outfit | **Student of Terrasea** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Limited Time Window">
Shinku's banner is only active for Phase 1 of Version 1.2. Ensure you complete your pulls before July 29, 2026, as these specific milestone rewards are tied to the limited board duration.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Blushing Mirage: Arc Pity and Research">

The **NTE Shinku pity** discussion isn't complete without mentioning her signature S-Class Arc, **Blushing Mirage**. This Condensate-type Arc is specifically designed to complement Shinku's skill set and is available through the "Resolve Special Arc Research Program." This program uses a separate currency—Tri-Keys or Resolve Keys—and runs concurrently with the character banner.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Arc Shop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Resolve Special">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check the Pool">
