<Section icon={Trophy} title="Scarborough Fair Gacha & Milestone Rewards">

Neverness to Everness features a unique board-game-style gacha system called the Scarborough Fair. Instead of a standard single-tap pull, players roll dice to move across a board containing characters, Arcs, and upgrade currencies. 

To incentivize players pulling on the **NTE Shinku banner**, the developers have introduced the Before the Dawn Resolve Perks. Reaching specific pull milestones guarantees high-tier cosmetics, though players also have a random chance to land on these rewards during their regular rolls.

| Roll Milestone | Reward Name | Item Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **50 Rolls** | Overcast Canopy | Glider Livery | Custom glider skin with flame visual accents |
| **120 Rolls** | Hidden Dragon | Blizzard-V4 Livery | Exclusive motorcycle vehicle skin |
| **200 Rolls** | Student of Terrasea | Character Outfit | Shinku's premium school-themed cosmetic skin |

<Callout type="success" title="Milestone Tip">
The milestone rewards are cumulative. Reaching 200 total rolls on the Before the Dawn board guarantees all three premium cosmetics, alongside any S-Class or A-Class characters recruited along the way.
</Callout>

</Section>
