<Section icon={Shield} title="Progression Guide: Unlocking Shinku's Potential">

Building the ultimate **NTE Shinku Iroi team** requires more than just pulling the character. You must progress through the Version 1.2 story content to unlock her full suite of interactions, including City Hangouts and Home Time. These features not only provide lore but also increase your Bond Level, which eventually unlocks the exclusive Labyrinth Lily outfit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Acquisition">
    Roll on the Before the Dawn Limited Board. Aim for the 50-roll milestone to at least secure the Overcast Canopy glider livery as a baseline reward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Arc Research">
    Access the Resolve Special Arc Research Program. Use Tri-Keys or Resolve Keys to target the Blushing Mirage Arc to unlock Shinku's true damage potential.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Story Integration">
    Complete the "Fighting with a Dragon" episode. This requires Hunter Level 26 and unlocks the Warren Continent, where Shinku's upgrade materials are found.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bond Building">
    Engage in City Hangouts and Home Time. Reaching Bond Level 10 is necessary to unlock her Labyrinth Lily costume and special passenger seat interactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Material farming is also a critical component. Players should focus on gathering Beetle Coins and Rising Hunter Guides through the 999 Nights limited-time event. These resources are essential for leveling Shinku's skills and ascending her Arc.
