<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Shinku and Progression">

Unlocking Shinku and integrating her into your roster involves a specific progression path within Version 1.2. Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss out on her limited-time content.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Prologue">
    You must finish the quest "Prologue - A New Destination - Head to Eibon Antique Shop." This is the hard requirement to open the banner menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Pull Resources">
    Collect Annulith from the Appreciation Gift (1,600) and Maintenance Compensation (600). Use active codes like DRAGONGIRL for extra currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Limited Board">
    Navigate to the Before the Dawn Limited Board. This is where Shinku is featured at an increased drop rate until July 29, 2026.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Engage in Story Content">
    Play the new episode "Fighting with a Dragon." This requires Hunter Level 26 and introduces the Warren Continent, Shinku's primary lore setting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Develop Character Bond">
    Participate in City Hangouts and Home Time invitations. Reaching Bond Level 10 unlocks her exclusive Labyrinth Lily outfit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Shinku's progression is tied closely to the "999 Nights" event. By participating in the event mini-games, you can earn additional materials like Elite Hunter Guides and Chaotic Dye, which are essential for her late-game development.
