### The Chemistry of Charge Reactions

A Charge reaction is a multi-step elemental process in Neverness to Everness. To activate it, your team must trigger both **Remora** and **Blossom** reactions:

| Reaction Type | Elemental Combination | Combat Effect on Enemies |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Remora** | Cosmos + Lakshana | Applies a persistent mark, boosting team CRIT Rate against targets |
| **Blossom** | Cosmos + Anima | Deals AoE damage and reduces target elemental resistances |
| **Charge** | Remora + Blossom | Grants massive team-wide Ultimate Energy and triggers AoE explosions |

By building a team containing Shinku (Cosmos), a Lakshana character, and an Anima character, you establish a self-sustaining energy loop. Every time a Charge reaction occurs, Shinku receives a massive refund of Ultimate Energy, allowing her to cycle back into her Surging Crimson state with minimal downtime.

To maximize your damage output, you must execute your character swaps in a precise order. This rotation ensures that all buffs, resistance shreds, and elemental marks are active before Shinku enters her Ultimate state.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Initial Energy">
    Begin the battle with **Shinku**. Use her Basic Attack string and Skill to build up her initial Esper Cycle gauge and Defiant Spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply the Lakshana Mark">
    Swap to **Hathor**. Cast her Ultimate and Skill to apply the Remora mark to all targets, immediately boosting your team's CRIT Rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Blossom & Set Buffs">
    Swap to **Zero** (or your Anima support like **Iroi**). Trigger the Blossom reaction using their Ultimate and Skill to shred enemy defenses and activate healing passives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Group and Shred">
    Rotate to your crowd control unit, such as **Jiuyuan** or **Nanally**. Use their abilities to gather the debuffed enemies tightly together.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unleash Crimson Ascension">
    Swap back to **Shinku**. Unleash her Ultimate to enter her transformed **Surging Crimson** state, then execute her enhanced melee combos to shred the grouped targets.
  </Step>
</Steps>
