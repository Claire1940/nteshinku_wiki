<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Shinku's Dual-Form Combat Kit">

To execute optimal **NTE Shinku rotations**, players must understand that she operates with two entirely different combat kits. In her normal form, her primary objective is to build her **Defiant Spirit** gauge. Once this gauge is fully charged, she can activate her Ultimate, **Crimson Fury**, to enter the 13-second **Surging Crimson** state (also referred to as Crimson Ascension). During this transformation window, her basic attacks and skills are replaced by significantly stronger, high-multiplier variants.

| Ability Name | Form | Core Combat Function | Defiant Spirit Gain |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Special Combat Arts** | Normal | 5-hit basic martial arts string; holding attacks triggers altered combos | Medium-High |
| **High-Speed Breach** | Normal | Redirect Skill; dashes forward dealing Cosmos DMG | Moderate |
| **Menacing Gaze** | Normal | Passive; stacks up to 8 times when off-field or not attacking | None (Grants DMG buff) |
| **Crimson Fury (Ultimate)** | Normal | Consumes Defiant Spirit to enter **Surging Crimson** state for 13s | Consumes all |
| **Altered Basic Attacks** | Surging Crimson | High-speed claws; generates Lucid Edge energy | N/A (Consumes Fading Reason) |
| **Crimson Judgment** | Surging Crimson | Rapid dash attacks triggered via the Ultimate button | N/A (Ultimate Finisher) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Menacing Gaze Stacking Rule">
If Shinku is on the field and does not attack for more than 2 seconds, she enters the **Menacing Gaze** state, gaining 1 stack per second (and 1 extra stack per Perfect Dodge) up to an 8-stack limit. Each stack increases her next attack's bonus damage by 100%. At base Awakening, swapping characters clears these stacks. However, at **Awakening 3**, she retains these stacks off-field and can even build them while benched, which completely changes how you structure your support setup!
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Shinku Combat Rotation">

Executing Shinku's rotations requires shifting smoothly between her resource-building phase and her ultimate burst window. In general, you want to spend as little time as possible on your supports to ensure Shinku doesn't lose her stacking attack buffs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Defiant Spirit">
    Begin the fight on Shinku. Spam her **Special Combat Arts** basic attack string. Weave in her **High-Speed Breach** Redirect Skill. Ensure you land the 3rd or 5th hit, or hold down the basic attack during the 3rd, 4th, or 5th hits to trigger the Altered combo variant for maximum Defiant Spirit generation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cycle Support Buffs">
    Once Shinku's Defiant Spirit gauge is full, swap to your support characters. Trigger their elemental skills, ultimates, and off-field abilities to set up reactions like **Remora** and **Blossom**. Do not spend more than 5–6 seconds on support setups.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Crimson Fury">
    Swap back to Shinku and immediately press her Ultimate to activate **Crimson Fury**. This triggers her transformation into the **Surging Crimson** state, boosting her interruption resistance, movement speed, and Cosmos damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Burst Combo">
    While in her transformed state, execute her enhanced damage loop: spam **Enhanced Basic Attacks** to deal massive damage and generate Lucid Edge energy, then cast her **Enhanced Redirect Skill**. You should aim to get at least two enhanced skill casts off during this window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unleash the Finisher">
    When her transformation timer is close to ending, continuously tap the Ultimate button to trigger **Crimson Judgment**. Shinku will dart between targets at blinding speed. Finish the combo with her final burst, **Ultimate Dragonflame Verdict**, to deal massive AoE Cosmos damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rotation Warning">
Avoid staying on your support characters for too long. If Shinku remains off-field for an extended duration, she may run out of her stacking attack buffs, or you may fail to fit all of her enhanced skill casts into her ultimate window.
</Callout>

</Section>
