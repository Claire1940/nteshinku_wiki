Shinku is a powerhouse Cosmos S-Class on-field damage dealer. Shinku relies heavily on the **Charge reaction** to fuel her Ultimate, which triggers her devastating **Crimson Ascension** transformation.

Because Shinku must remain on the field to deal damage and maintain her buffs, her teammates must be able to apply elements and trigger reactions from off-field.

### The Charge Reaction Formula
To keep Shinku's Ultimate uptime near 100%, your team must consistently cycle through these elemental reactions:

| Reaction Name | Elements Required | Combat Effect on Enemies |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Remora** | Cosmos + Lakshana | Debuffs target and enables the setup for Charge reactions. |
| **Blossom** | Cosmos + Anima | Deals AoE damage and sets up the second half of Charge. |
| **Charge** | Remora + Blossom | Refunds massive Ultimate Energy to the active character and triggers AoE explosions. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Shinku's passive, **Lone Stride**, deals 400% ATK as AoE damage when Charge is triggered while she is active, while also stacking an ATK buff up to 10 times. Never trigger Charge while your supports are on-field!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Rotation Guide">

To maximize your damage output, you must execute your character switches in a precise order. This ensures that all debuffs are active and that Shinku is the one who benefits from the Charge energy refunds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Shinku's Initial Meter">
    Start the fight with **Shinku**. Perform basic attacks and skills to build her initial Esper Cycle meter and Defiant Spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Remora with Hathor">
    Switch to **Hathor**. Cast her Ultimate and then her Skill to apply the Remora debuff to enemies and trigger her CRIT Rate buff.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Generate Cosmos Energy with Zero">
    Switch to **Zero**. Cast their Ultimate and Skill to quickly fill the Cosmos Esper Cycle gauge and prepare the team's defensive heals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger Blossom with Nanally">
    Switch to **Nanally** (or Jiuyuan). Use her Skill and Ultimate, then perform Basic Attacks for 6 seconds to trigger Blossom and group enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unleash Crimson Ascension">
    Switch back to **Shinku**. Immediately cast her Ultimate to enter her transformed Crimson Ascension state, then spam her enhanced skills and martial combos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Rotation Benefit">
Following this rotation ensures that when Shinku triggers Charge, Zero's **Appraiser** skill heals her for 50% of Zero's Base ATK, keeping her safe during her aggressive close-range combat combos.
</Callout>
