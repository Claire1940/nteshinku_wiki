<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Steps for Skill and Arc Enhancement">

Upgrading Shinku's skills and her signature Arc, **Blushing Mirage**, is just as important as her base level. Skill upgrades require specialized materials often found in the **Resolve Special Arc Research Program** or high-level combat tutorials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Skill Unlock">
    Complete the in-game combat tutorial for Shinku to unlock her basic skill tree. This provides a small amount of starter materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Arc Synchronization">
    Equip the **Blushing Mirage** Arc. Use **Tri-Keys** or **Resolve Keys** to pull for duplicates or upgrade materials in the Arc Research menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Skill Leveling (1-4)">
    Use **Rising Hunter Guides** and **Beetle Coins** to upgrade her basic attack and secondary skills to level 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Advanced Skill Leveling (5-10)">
    Transition to using **Elite Hunter Guides** and **Chaotic Dyes**. These levels provide the most significant percentage increases to her damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Bond Level Milestones">
    Reach **Bond Level 10** to unlock the **Labyrinth Lily** outfit and final skill nodes, which require unique Bond materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Arc Priority">
The **Blushing Mirage** Arc is a Condensate-type (formerly Synthesis-type) weapon. It is highly recommended to prioritize its level alongside Shinku's to benefit from the stat synergy.
</Callout>

</Section>
