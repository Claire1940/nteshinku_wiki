<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Materials in Version 1.2">

Farming **NTE Shinku ascension materials** effectively requires a structured approach to your daily and weekly gameplay loop. With the introduction of the Warren Continent, new farming nodes have appeared that offer higher drop rates for Dyes and Hunter Guides.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Warren Continent">
    Complete the main story episode "Fighting with a Dragon" by reaching Hunter Level 26. This continent contains the elite enemies necessary for Rank 5 and 6 breakthrough materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear 999 Nights Mini-Games">
    Participate in the seasonal mini-games to earn "Button Shop" currency. This currency can be traded for Chaotic Dyes and Elite Hunter Guides at a much lower cost than the standard shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Daily Domain Rotation">
    Spend your energy on the "Hunter's Trial" domains. Rotate between the Cosmos-themed domains on Tuesdays and Fridays to maximize the specific drops needed for Shinku's skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Eibon Antique Shop Trade-ins">
    Visit the Eibon Antique Shop daily to check for "Beetle Coin" trade-ins. You can often exchange common monster drops for the rarer Colorless Dyes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficient Route">
The northern cliffs of Warren Continent feature a high density of "Cosmos Anomalies" which drop both Dyes and Hunter Guides simultaneously. This is currently the most time-efficient farming route in Version 1.2.
