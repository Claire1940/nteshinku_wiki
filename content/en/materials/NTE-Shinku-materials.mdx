<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Prefarm & Progression Guide">

To ensure Shinku is combat-ready the moment you pull her, follow this structured prefarming route. This guide focuses on maximizing your efficiency during the first week of Version 1.2.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Warren Continent">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Event Currency">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Redeem All Active Codes">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Shinku's Combat Tutorial">
    Version 1.2 adds a dedicated tutorial for Shinku. Completing this not only teaches you her timing and "dragon girl" mechanics but also rewards you with basic upgrade materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Push Bond Levels">
    Once you have Shinku, engage in City Hangouts and Home Time. Reaching Bond Level 10 unlocks the **Labyrinth Lily** outfit, which is a free alternative to her shop skins.
  </Step>
</Steps>
