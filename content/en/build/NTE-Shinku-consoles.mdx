<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Shinku Module Optimization & Stats">

Modules are critical components of Shinku's console setup, allowing you to fine-tune her base stats. Her console passive grants a massive specialized bonus: Shinku gains **16% CRIT DMG for every Type III Module** equipped. 

To min-max her build, you should aim to slot exactly four Type III Modules. This yields a permanent passive boost of **64% extra CRIT DMG**.

### Module Stat Priorities
When farming and upgrading Modules for Shinku's console, prioritize the following stat hierarchy:

1. **CRIT Rate** (Main Stat / Substat) — Crucial to ensure her high-damage Ultimate strikes land critically.
2. **CRIT DMG** (Substat) — Scales alongside her console passive for monumental burst spikes.
3. **ATK% / Flat ATK** (Substat) — Increases her baseline scaling.
4. **Cosmos DMG%** (Main Stat on specialized slots) — Multiplies her overall elemental damage output.

```
[Type III Module Slot 1: Lost Radiance] ──> +16% CRIT DMG
[Type III Module Slot 2: Lost Radiance] ──> +16% CRIT DMG
[Type III Module Slot 3: Free Slot]     ──> +16% CRIT DMG
[Type III Module Slot 4: Free Slot]     ──> +16% CRIT DMG
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Total Console Specialization Bonus:         +64% CRIT DMG
```

<Callout type="warning" title="CRIT Balancing Tip">
Do not neglect CRIT Rate in favor of pure CRIT DMG. Aim for a healthy **1:2 ratio** (e.g., 60% CRIT Rate to 120%+ CRIT DMG) to make sure her console passive isn't wasted on non-critical hits.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Rotation & Mechanics">

Shinku's combat loop is split into a normal gathering phase and an explosive transformation phase. She relies on **Defiant Spirit** instead of standard Ultimate Energy to activate her transformation, meaning you must actively engage in her specific mechanics to build her gauge.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Deterrent Gaze Stacks">
    When Shinku is in combat but remains idle (not attacking) for 2 seconds, she enters the Menacing Gaze state, gaining 1 stack per second up to a cap of 8. Perfect Dodges also award instant stacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unleash Instant Strike">
    Execute a Basic Attack to consume all 8 stacks. This triggers an **Instant Strike** that deals massive damage and rapidly fills her Defiant Spirit gauge, Skill energy, and Cycle Meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Setup & Reactions">
    Swap to your support units (such as Hathor and Nanally) to trigger the **Charge** reaction. Shinku's passive ensures that all allies gain Ultimate energy from Charge reactions while she is in the team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger Surging Crimson">
    Swap back to Shinku and cast her Ultimate to enter the **Surging Crimson** state. This activates her Cartridge's 25% DEF ignore and her signature Arc's 32% Cosmos DMG buff.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute Transformed Combo">
    Perform her enhanced basic attacks to build **Fading Reason**. Use her enhanced skill up to 5 times, followed by her 3 Ultimate dashes, culminating in her devastating Ultimate finisher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
