<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Skill Mastery">

Shinku's combat identity is defined by her "awkward but dependable" nature. In the heat of battle, she transforms from a reserved dragon girl into the first line of defense for her team. To master the **NTE Shinku build**, you must understand her combat rhythm, which blends heavy-hitting Cosmos attacks with protective maneuvers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Cosmos Esper Skills">
    Use her primary skills to apply Cosmos-type pressure on anomalies. This builds the necessary energy for her ultimate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Protective Stance">
    Shinku excels at stepping into danger. Use her defensive utility when teammates are low on health or facing high-impact boss attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Combat Tutorial">
    Version 1.2 includes a dedicated combat tutorial for Shinku. Completing this is mandatory for understanding her specific timing and dodge-cancel windows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize with A-Class Supports">
    Switch to characters like **Mint** or **Edgar** to trigger elemental or physical reactions that amplify Shinku's Cosmos damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Mastery">
Shinku's ultimate ability is most effective when the enemy's shield is broken. Coordinate your team's rotation to ensure Shinku delivers the final blow or the heaviest burst during the break window.
</Callout>
