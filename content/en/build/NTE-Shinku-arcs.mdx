### Blushing Mirage: The Ultimate DPS Catalyst

Her signature S-Class Arc, **Blushing Mirage**, is tailor-made to support her burst rotation. Because Shinku’s damage concentrates heavily within her enhanced Surging Crimson state after activating her Ultimate, the weapon's passive trigger window perfectly aligns with her combat flow.

| Attribute / Effect | Value & Description | Utility Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base ATK** | 570 (At Max Level) | ★★★★★ |
| **Substat** | 24% CRIT Rate | ★★★★★ |
| **ATK Boost** | Increases base ATK by 20% passively | ★★★★☆ |
| **Cosmos DMG Buff** | +32% Cosmos DMG for 20s after casting Ultimate | ★★★★★ |
| **DEF Ignore** | Ignores 12% of enemy DEF for 20s after Ultimate | ★★★★★ |

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy Tip">

</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Story Prerequisites">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Arc Research">
    Open your in-game menu, head to the **Arc Shop**, select the **Arc Research Program**, and locate the **Resolve Special** banner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Convert Your Gacha Currency">
    Ensure you have **Tri-Keys** or **Resolve Keys** ready. These are the exclusive currencies required to pull on the limited weapon banner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Your Path and Pull">
    Confirm that **Blushing Mirage** is the selected target weapon on the Resolve Special board before spending your keys.
  </Step>
</Steps>
