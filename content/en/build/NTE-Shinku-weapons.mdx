<Section icon={Swords} title="NTE Shinku Weapons Overview">

In *Neverness to Everness* (NTE), weapons are known as **Arcs**. For an S-Class Cosmos Esper like Shinku, choosing the correct Arc is essential to unlocking her true damage ceiling. Shinku is a highly build-dependent on-field DPS who deals massive burst damage during her Ultimate's Rampage state. 

Without a proper weapon, her armor-shredding and Cosmos multipliers fall off significantly. This guide breaks down her absolute best signature options, alternative S-Class Arcs, and budget-friendly F2P weapons to ensure your Shinku is ready to shred any anomaly in Hethereau.

<Callout type="tip" title="Weapon Compatibility Notice">
Remember that characters in NTE can only equip Arcs that match their elemental or combat type. Shinku uses a **Condensate-type Arc** (also referred to as Synthesis-type Arc in earlier builds), meaning you must match her with compatible options to activate passive weapon effects.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Steps} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Shinku's Weapons">

Upgrading your Arcs is vital to keeping pace with high-level world anomalies and boss fights. Follow these steps to unlock, equip, and ascend Shinku's weapons.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Limited Arc Program">
    Complete **Prologue - A New Destination - Head to Eibon Antique Shop** to unlock the weapon gacha. Navigate to **Arc Shop > Arc Research Program > Resolve Special** and use Tri-Keys or Resolve Keys to pull for *Blushing Mirage*.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Arc via Character Menu">
    Open your in-game menu, select Shinku's character profile, and navigate to the **Arc** tab. Equip your selected Condensate-type weapon to activate its base stats and unique passive effects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Leveling Dyes">
    Use **Dyes** (such as Light, Colorless, or Chaotic Dyes obtained from codes and daily anomalies) to feed EXP into your weapon and raise its level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascend to Increase Level Caps">
    Gather colored ascension materials corresponding to your weapon's tier. Note that weapon ascensions are locked behind your **Appraisal Level**, which increases every 10 Hunter Levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>
