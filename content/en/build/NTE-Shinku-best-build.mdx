Shinku enters *Neverness to Everness* (NTE) Version 1.2 as an elite S-Class Cosmos Esper who dominates the field as a primary on-field hypercarry. Her unique combat identity revolves around a dual-form mechanic: she builds up **Defiant Spirit** in her normal state using rapid-fire martial arts strings, then unleashes her Ultimate to enter the **Surging Crimson** (or Rising Crimson) state. In this empowered state, her moveset transforms into a flurry of high-mobility, high-damage Cosmos attacks. 

To unlock Shinku's true damage ceiling, your build must focus heavily on stacking **DEF ignore** and **CRIT stats**. When her signature weapon's passive, her cartridge set bonus, and her natural multipliers overlap, Shinku bypasses enemy armor to deal near-true damage.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Cartridge & Module Setup">

The Cartridge and Module system is where you build Shinku's raw stats. There is only one competitive Cartridge set for her, and it revolves around amplifying her post-Ultimate burst window.

### The Lost Radiance Cartridge Set
The **Lost Radiance** (also known as Lost Light) set is non-negotiable for an optimal Shinku build. It directly scales her elemental damage and rewards her for casting her Ultimate.

- **2-Piece Bonus**: Increases Cosmos / Light DMG by 10%.
- **4-Piece Bonus**: After casting an Ultimate, the wearer's damage ignores 25% of the enemy's DEF for 20 seconds (does not stack).

### Optimal Module Selection
Modules provide substantial secondary stats. For Shinku, you want to target **Type III Modules** to gain massive passive bonuses.

| Module Slot | Recommended Type | Targeted Main Stat | Optimal Substats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slot 1 & 2** | Lost Radiance (Type III) | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, Cycle Intensity |
| **Slot 3 & 4** | Chaos DMG (Type III) | Cosmos DMG% | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, DMG%, ATK% |

Shinku's combat loop requires active management of her gauges. Upgrading her skills in the correct order ensures you aren't wasting materials on low-impact multipliers.

### Skill Upgrade Order
Following her post-release balance changes, Shinku's optimal upgrade priority is:

  <Step num="1" title="Basic Attack: Special Combat Arts">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Ultimate: Crimson Fury / Crimson Execution">
    Scales the damage of her transformation, her dash attacks, and her massive Dragonflame finisher.

  </Step>

### The Perfect Combat Rotation
To maximize her damage on the field, execute the following team and character rotation:

| Step | Character | Action / Input | Combat Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Shinku** | Basic Attacks & High-Speed Breach | Fill her Esper Cycle and Defiant Spirit gauge. |
| **2** | **Hathor** | Cast Ultimate, then cast Redirect Skill | Apply Remora debuff and trigger Charge reactions. |
| **3** | **Zero (MC)** | Cast Ultimate, then cast Skill | Fill team energy and prepare for Blossom reactions. |
